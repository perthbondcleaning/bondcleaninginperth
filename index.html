What We Offer

Complete Bond Cleaning Services

End of Lease / Bond Cleaning

Our flagship service covers the entire property from top to bottom following the official REIWA vacate cleaning checklist — the standard every real estate agent and property manager expects.

  • Skirtings, door frames, light switches & power points

  • Removal of cobwebs throughout

Bathroom & Toilet Deep Clean

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your end of lease clean in Perth.

What does bond cleaning include?

Our standard bond clean covers all interior rooms including kitchen, bathrooms, bedrooms, living areas, laundry, and outdoor areas. We clean all surfaces, fixtures, fittings, windows, and floors. Carpet steam cleaning is included as an optional add-on.

How much does bond cleaning cost in Perth?

Pricing depends on the size of the property and its current condition. A 1-bedroom unit typically starts from $250, while larger homes are quoted individually. Contact us for a free, no-obligation quote — we always provide upfront flat-rate pricing with no hidden fees.

How long does bond cleaning take?

A standard 2-bedroom unit takes approximately 4–6 hours. A 4-bedroom house may take 7–10 hours. We send a team sized appropriately for your property to ensure everything is completed efficiently and to the highest standard within your required timeframe.

Do I need to be present during the clean?

No, you don't need to be present. Many of our clients hand us a key or provide access codes and we complete the job independently. We'll notify you when the clean is finished and send a detailed receipt so you can share it with your property manager.

Is carpet steam cleaning included?

Carpet steam cleaning is available as an add-on service and is highly recommended, as most WA lease agreements require it. We use professional hot water extraction equipment and provide a separate receipt for carpets, which many landlords require as evidence of professional cleaning.

What is your re-clean guarantee?

If your property manager identifies any area we missed or that doesn't meet inspection standards, contact us within 72 hours of the inspection and we'll return and rectify the issues at absolutely no charge. Our goal is for you to receive your full bond — guaranteed.

Do you clean rental properties in all Perth suburbs?

Yes, we cover the entire Perth Metropolitan Area including northern suburbs, southern suburbs, eastern suburbs, and inner-city areas. There is no additional travel charge for any suburb within the metro region. Call us to confirm availability in your area.

Can I book for a weekend or public holiday?

Absolutely. We operate 7 days a week, including weekends and most public holidays. We understand that lease end dates rarely fall conveniently on weekdays, so we're available whenever you need us. Same-day bookings are available subject to team availability.

What cleaning products do you use?

We use commercial-grade, eco-certified cleaning products that are highly effective yet safe for your family and pets. Our degreasers, descalers, and sanitisers are the same grade used by professional property maintenance companies — far stronger than anything available in retail stores.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking as soon as you know your vacate date — ideally 3–5 days in advance to secure your preferred time slot. However, we understand things can be last-minute, and we'll always do our best to accommodate same-day or next-day bookings when possible.
